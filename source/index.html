About Us

Home or Office - You Pick! car wash You pick the location, day and time and we'll take care of the rest. We'll show up on-time with our state-of-the-art mobile van loaded with all of the bells and whistles! You don't even have to provide us with water or electricity. Our van has it all! Wash on Wheels provides the highest quality services and guarantees complete customer satisfaction! ​ Wash on Wheels was founded by two brothers, who have a tremendous passion for cars and more importantly, keeping them clean! ​ Running out of patience waiting at detail shops for their cars to be cleaned, they decided to start Wash on Wheels. They have no problem traveling to your home or office to remove the hassle of having to get your car detailed!

Services

What Can We Do For You?

Wash on Wheels simplifies the auto detailing experience by coming to you! We make getting your car detailed, simple, cost-effective and most of all, convenient.

  • Full Wash
    Cars: $35 || SUV: $45

    This is your basic full wash service that will spruce up your vehicle. Interior and Exterior:

    Hand wash and dry || Clean all windows
    Dust off interior || Full interior vacuum including trunk
    Clean and dress wheels, tires and rims

  • Wash & Shine
    Cars: $55 || SUV: $75

    Quality hand wash and spray gloss that will give your vehicle a just waxed look. This process is recommended for vehicles that already have a good base wax. The interior will be shining as well:

    Full wash plus spray gloss &
    interior premium dressing || Apply spray gloss wax with micro fiber towel

  • Wash & Wax
    Cars: $100 || SUV: $125

    Quality hand wash and wax service that completely cleans your car and leaves a lasting shine on your finish.

    Exterior wash plus carnauba wax

  • Gold Package MOST POPULAR
    Cars: $150 || SUV: $175

    Our complete detailing service, which is designed to make your vehicle look as close to new as possible
    Full wash plus:

    Hand wax exterior with a quality wax || Hot water extract cloth upholstery and floor mat
    Clean and dress vinyl surfaces || Clean and condition leather surfaces || Clean and protect dashboard (U.V. Block)
    Clean dashboard cup holders and ash trays || Clean all door jambs, vents, crevices etc.

  • Platinum Package
    Cars: $225 || SUV: $250

    Our ultimate detailing service, which is designed to make it look like it just came out of the showroom.
    Full wash plus:

    Clay bar exterior (to remove surface contaminants) || Paint Sealant (long term protection)
    High speed polish (extreme shine) || Apply extended spray gloss || Hot water extract all cloth upholstery and floor mats || Clean door jambs, vents, crevices etc. || Clean and condition vinyl and leather surfaces || Clean dashboard cup holders and ashtrays
