About Us

  • Who Are We?

    Wash on Wheels was founded by two brothers, who have a tremendous passion for cars and more importantly, keeping them clean!
    ​
    Running out of patience waiting at detail shops for their cars to be cleaned, they decided to start Wash on Wheels. They have no problem traveling to your home or office to remove the hassle of having to get your car detailed!

  • How Does It Work?

    You pick the location, day and time and we'll take care of the rest. We'll show up on-time with our state-of-the-art mobile van loaded with all of the bells and whistles! You don't even have to provide us with water or electricity. Our van has it all!
    ​
    Wash on Wheels provides the highest quality services and guarantees complete customer satisfactio
